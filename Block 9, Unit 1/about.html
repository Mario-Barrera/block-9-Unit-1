My journey’s been a unique blend of heart and heat 21 years in nursing and
5 years in the culinary world. I’ve worked in home health, hospice, and hospital
settings, where I learned the true meaning of care and human connection. That same
empathy and attention to detail now fuel my second passion: food.

As a chef, I craft experiences, not just meals. From intimate weddings to milestone celebrations,
I take pride in bringing people together through the power of food. Cooking is more than just a
skill—it's a form of caregiving. Whether it’s perfecting the flaky crust of a gourmet pie or creating
a memorable dish for hundreds, I believe that food is about comfort, culture, and connection.
Every dish I prepare is made with care, precision, and pride, because great food, like great care,
is all in the details.

Outside of work, you’ll usually find me experimenting in the kitchen, enjoying time with family,
or dreaming up my next big event.

Thanks for swinging by, I’m thrilled to have you here! If your taste buds are feeling adventurous, let me
know and I’ll save you a slice of my latest pie masterpiece.
