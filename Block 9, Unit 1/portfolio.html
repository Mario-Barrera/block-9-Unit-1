Caregiver by heart, chef by passion

For over 21 years, I’ve been dedicated to taking care of people. My journey in nursing has taken me through
hospitals, hospice care, home health, and just about every setting you can imagine. Whether I was holding a hand
in a quiet moment or helping someone heal, I gave it my all—because that’s what people deserve.

But my story doesn’t stop at the bedside.

About five years ago, I followed another passion: cooking. And just like nursing, it’s all about care—only this time,
it’s served on a plate. My goal is to nourish both body and spirit. I have seen events - from cozy family gatherings to joyful
celebrations - as an opportunity to share comfort, culture, and creativity through food. Whether I’m baking a tender,
flaky pie from scratch or plating a meal for a hundred guests, I approach it with intention and heart. Food, to me,
is a way of caring—it’s in the details, the flavors, and the connections it creates around the table.

What ties it all together—both as a nurse and a chef—is my passion for people. I care deeply. I listen. I give my
full energy and attention to every patient, every dish, every guest. Whether it’s creating a moment of healing
or serving a plate of joy, I show up with heart and intention.

I hold an Associate of Applied Science in Nursing and an Associate of Applied Science in Culinary Arts.
And I believe every bit of that learning has shaped me into who I am today—a creative, compassionate
professional who brings skill, soul, and a dash of spice to everything I do.

Thanks for stopping by. I’m so glad you’re here. Let’s connect soon—whether you need care, a chef, or just a good conversation.

— Mario
